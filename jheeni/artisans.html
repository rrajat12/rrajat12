Meet the Artisans

Aashiq Hussain

Aashiq Hussain is the 3rd generation in the family practicing, the craft of Kota Doria. Aashig Hussain's mother is the National Awardee 1996-1997 (27 states). In 2010 she was awatded the Maharana Prasid honour and in 2019 she was awarded by the state of Rajasthan. Aashiq has a younger brother who works in the bank and a sister who works as an Inspector.

Iaftikhar Husain

Iaftikhar Hussain and his younger brother are the 3rd generation craftspeople with very young sons whom he wishes would grow up and carry the craft forward. He lives in Kota and works from home. His house is his Kota Doria workshop and has the loom as well, he works along with 5o other Karigars each involved in different steps of the process.
