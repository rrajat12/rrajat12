Kota Doria

Kota Doria Craft originates from Rajasthan, Kota, where artisans have been practicing this craft for generations. The word 'Dori' literally translated from Hindi, means threads'.

Kethun, a village of about 15,000 people in Rajasthan, is home to the ethereal Kota Doria weave. The fabric is transparent and light suitable for daily wear throughout the year for Indian people. Over the years the craft has grown its range of products and designs.
